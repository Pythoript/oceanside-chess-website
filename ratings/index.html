Ratings

Learn more about our local rating system, how it works, and why we developed it.

The Ratings

While there are many organizations that offer to rate games, such as FIDE or CFC, there is always a cost. There is a cost to register, each tournament has to be approved, and there is much hassle in paperwork and membership fees. As a result of this, we began to develop our own rating system based on the latest Glicko rating system.
